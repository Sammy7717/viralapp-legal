1) What we collect

1.1 Data you authorize via TikTok Login

We request the scopes user.info.basic and video.list. From video.list we process: id, create_time, duration, view_count, like_count, comment_count, share_count. We do not access private messages, drafts, or audience identifiers.

1.2 App usage data

Operational logs (timestamps, errors, device/OS, timezone) to keep the service secure and reliable.

1.3 Optional inputs

Local weather/holidays and optional CSV uploads are processed only to generate posting recommendations.

We do not intentionally collect special category data.

2) Why we use your data (purposes & legal bases)

  • Provide the service (ViralScore, best day/hour, reports) — Legal basis: Contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).
  • Improve & secure the product (debugging, fraud prevention, aggregate stats) — Legal basis: Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)).
  • Optional features (notifications, location-based suggestions, AI explanations) — Legal basis: Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)).

We do not sell your data.

3) Tokens & security

Access/refresh tokens are stored server-side, encrypted in transit and at rest; least-privilege access and key rotation apply. On disconnect or deletion request, tokens are revoked and purged.

4) Data sharing (processors)

  • Google Cloud Platform (EU regions: europe-west3 Frankfurt / europe-west1 Belgium) — hosting & storage.
  • OpenAI — optional short text insights; aggregated stats only, no media/personal identifiers.
  • Weather/Holidays APIs (e.g., OpenWeather, Calendarific) — we may send country/lat-long or dates.
  • Error/analytics tools — pseudonymous technical logs.

Vendors are bound by contracts/confidentiality. International transfers use SCCs or equivalent safeguards.

5) Retention

  • TikTok video metrics: up to 24 months (or shorter if you delete or request erasure).
  • Tokens: deleted on disconnect or after 30 days of inactivity.
  • Server logs: ~90 days.

6) Your rights (GDPR/EEA)

You can access, correct, delete, restrict, or export your data, and object to certain processing. To exercise rights or withdraw consent: samdely@gmail.com. You may also contact the Hellenic DPA (www.dpa.gr).

7) Children

Not directed to children; do not use if under the digital consent age in your country.

8) How to disconnect & delete

In TikTok → Settings → Security → Apps & websites, remove access to “ViralApp”, or email samdely@gmail.com with subject “Account deletion”. We aim to complete deletion within 30 days.

9) Changes

We may update this Policy and post the new version with a new “Last updated” date.
